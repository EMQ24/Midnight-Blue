Press the Play button. Simon will repeat his first signal. If you didn't enable hard mode, the note played will be highlighted. Repeat the signal by pressing the same note.

Simon will repeat the first signal and add another. Repeat these two signals by pressing the same notes, in order.

Simon will repeat these first two signals and add yet another. Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of notes correctly. After every 3rd note in a sequence, Simon automatically speeds up. Click the bulb to toggle hard mode and the speaker to toggle non-piano sound effects.
